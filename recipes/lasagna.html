Classic Homemade Lasagna Recipe

Introduction:

Indulge in the comforting layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and gooey melted cheese with this classic homemade lasagna recipe. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions, this timeless dish is sure to satisfy your cravings. Follow our step-by-step instructions to create a mouthwatering lasagna that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients:

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1 pound (450g) Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Béchamel Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Other Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package instructions
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef and Italian sausage until browned. Drain excess fat.
    2. Add chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet and sauté until the onion is softened.
    3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, red wine (if using), oregano, basil, sugar, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  2. Make the Béchamel Sauce:
    1. In a medium saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Add flour and whisk continuously until a paste forms.
    2. Gradually whisk in the milk, nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Continue whisking until the sauce thickens, about 8-10 minutes. Remove from heat.

  3. Assemble the Lasagna:
    1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. In a greased 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce.
    3. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
    4. Spread a portion of the meat sauce over the noodles, followed by a layer of béchamel sauce and a sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
    5. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.

  4. Bake:
    1. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
    2. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

  5. Serve:
    1. Allow the lasagna to cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing.
    2. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.
    3. Serve and enjoy your homemade classic lasagna!

This timeless lasagna recipe is sure to be a hit at your next gathering. Buon appetito!
